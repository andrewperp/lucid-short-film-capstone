# Concept Development

## Project Idea

LUCID began as a short film concept centered on the feeling of uncertainty between dreams and waking life. I wanted the film to feel immersive, tense, and visually cinematic while still being emotionally readable.

## Themes

The project explores:
- perception versus reality
- unease and disorientation
- dream logic
- psychological tension
- the cinematic power of visual atmosphere

## Creative Direction

From the beginning, I wanted LUCID to feel polished and cinematic rather than purely experimental. The goal was to create a film that could function as both a narrative project and a portfolio piece that reflects my visual style.
